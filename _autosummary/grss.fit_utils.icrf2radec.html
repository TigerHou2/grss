grss.fit_utils.icrf2radec

grss.fit_utils.icrf2radec(pos_x, pos_y, pos_z, deg=False)

Convert ICRF unit vector to Right Ascension and Declination.

Parameters

  • pos_x (float or array) – ICRF unit vector x-component.

  • pos_y (float or array) – ICRF unit vector y-component.

  • pos_z (float or array) – ICRF unit vector z-component.

  • deg (bool, optional) – Flag for whether angles are in degrees, by default False

Returns

  • r_asc (float or array) – Right Ascension in degrees or radians.

  • dec (float or array) – Declination in degrees or radians.
